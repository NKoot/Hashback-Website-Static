Hashback is the only platform in existence that allows service providers to get paid twice — once for selling their services and once more by mining cryptocurrency.

Service providers can now earn tokens simply by registering with Hasback and listing their services on the blockchain.

Customers contract services directly with security and confidence thanks to the transparency, reputation and verification algorithms of the Hashback token.

Hashback is open source and programmable which means that new or existing loyalty and rewards programs can be built on top of or integrated into Hashback.

Service providers:
  • Mine tokens while you market your services and await sales orders
  • Sell your services quicker, faster and directly to your customer without 3rd party fees
  • Pocket additional money once you deliver your services
Users:
  • Contract services directly, securely and more conveniently
  • Buy services stripped of all 3rd party upcharges
  • Influence reputations and ratings like never before and earn rewards for it
Through a decentralized and autonomous mining process, service providers generate tokens whenever their orders are listed as available but have not been bought yet.
No computing power is needed to mine for tokens. Hashback’s app and blockchain technology do all the work so the service provider need only manage its own service availability.
Once the service is purchased the service provider delivers its service and collects both the sales revenue and now also the secondary mined revenue.
Bitcoin removed 3rd parties from financial transactions. Hashback removes 3rd parties from discontinuous and/or intermittent sales order services industries.
