Hashback is the first decentralized blockchain based token that converts time spent performing services into a digitized crypto-asset.

By turning time services into tokenized property Hashback eliminates intermediaries in areas where time itself is the actual service or product, or time-as-a-token. By virtue, the need for many third party intermediation services is eliminated and loyalty is built directly between the service provider and the customer.
